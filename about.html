Hey there!

I’m Zain, a student (on the right) at the Texas Academy of Math and Science studying to be a product designer. I once wanted to be a programmer, but decided that I preferred not coding.

I entered design when I went to a hacakthon and liked the free stuff, but not the code. Instead, I kept thinking of ideas and new ways to improve the lives of people around me.

I’ve previously worked with the UNT College of Engineering to create an accessible auditory display to enhance programming for visually impaired users.

I currently work with the xREZ Art + Science Lab at the University of North Texas, studying virtual presence. I'm also lucky to get the opportunity to visit many different places and write about my experiences on Medium.

Let me know if you’d like to talk or work with me!


Also, I’m pretty funny on Twitter.
