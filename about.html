What's up!

I’m Zain, a student at Cornell University studying Information Science.

I entered design when I went to a hackathon and enjoyed connecting people to ideas through the medium of user experiences and products. I'm always thinking of ideas and new ways to improve the lives of people around me, whether they include technology or not.

I've previously engaged in research at the University of North Texas (UNT) in various usability testing labs. Along with a team of dedicated students, I developed an accessible auditory display to enhance programming for visually impaired users. I also worked with the xREZ Art + Science Lab at the University of North Texas, where I studied virtual presence.

I look for new experiences and lifestyles everywhere I go, so be sure to catch a few of my write-ups on Medium.

If you'd like to get in touch with me, feel free to reach out via Twitter or LinkedIn. Let me know if you’d like to talk or work with me!
